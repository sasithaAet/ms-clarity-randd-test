How we roast

A short, honest process

No mystery blends, no warehouse stock. Every bag is roasted, bagged, and shipped on a schedule you can trace back to the harvest.

Step 01
Source
We buy from three growing regions directly, paying above fair-trade minimums each season. Relationships with growers run 4+ years deep, so pricing is agreed before harvest, not after.
Step 02
Roast
Batches stay under 12kg on a 1957 drum roaster, profiled per lot rather than by a fixed curve. Each roast is cupped same-day against a reference standard.
Step 03
Ship
Bagged and shipped within 48 hours of roast date — never from a warehouse shelf. Every bag carries the roast date printed on the seal.
